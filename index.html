  • The Ontario Student Assistance Program (OSAP) is a financial aid program that can help you pay for college or university.
  • OSAP is available for both part-time and full=time students.
  • OSAP offers funding through:
    • grants: money you don’t have to pay back.
    • a student loan: money you need to repay once you’re done school.
  • You are eligible for OSAP if you are:
    • Canadian Citizen
    • Permanent Resident, or
    • Protected Persons.
  • Money from OSAP can be used to pay for Tuition, books, fees, Living expenses, childcare.
